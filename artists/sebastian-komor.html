Sebastian Komor

Xenomorph Productions · Vancouver, BC · Represented by Ninja Jo Artworks

TV & film Trailers Industrial / EBM Cinematic Library production

An experienced electronic musician, composer, producer, and remixer. Active since the early 1990s and best known for his work with Icon of Coil, Sebastian has released a substantial production music catalog through Position Music spanning industrial, EBM, techno, and cinematic styles. His tracks have been placed in TV series such as Face Off, The Vampire Diaries, Smallville, and others, as well as high-profile trailers, commercials, and gaming projects.
