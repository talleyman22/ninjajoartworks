How it works

We confirm scope with the artist or producer before issuing a firm quote or agreement.

  1. Submit your request Tell us lane, scope, timeline, usage, and budget signal — same fields we use for agreements.
  2. Internal confirmation We run final details by the creator before quoting. You'll hear: confirmed scope and terms, not a blind estimate.
  3. Agreement & invoice Ninja Jo Artworks, Ltd Co handles contract execution and payment coordination.
  4. Delivery Creator delivers per agreed scope. Final art files release after full payment.
